Well hello there.

Welcome to the Portland Interdependent Chamber of Commerce.

Portland-based

PICOC is dedicated to ensuring that Portland businesses feel that their voices are being heard, and that as a community we're doing everything we can to support them.

Interdependent

We represent forward-thinking businesses that see the link between their success and Portland's status as an affordable, sustainable and equitable city. Portland is changing fast, and PICOC helps businesses ensure that it continues to be a great place to do business.

Businesses

A healthy civic community depends upon the social contribution of businesses. PICOC supports Portland-based businesses of any size - whether a sole-proprietor providing freelance design services, a 100 year old family-owned retail establishment, or a venture-backed startup.
